Meringues

by Tomas Ravinskas
Published on August 01, 2020, 04:59 AM
This seems difficult only until you try!

You'll need:

Amount
Ingredient
1
large egg white
80g
granulated sugar

Process

Whisk the egg white with sugar until it forms soft peaks (it's never gonna form stiff peaks because of all the sugar.) Scoop up the mass with teaspoon and place on baking sheet covered tray. (Or better yet, fill confectioners syringe and make pretty little puffs.) Bake for 25min at 180 degrees C.
One portion makes about half full-sized baking tray.
Via Recipe book my sister found in Germany
